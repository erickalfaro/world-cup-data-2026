# `ericks_squad/` — my team & my reasoning

This is the **personal** corner of the repo. Everything else (`data/`, `scripts/`) is reusable
by anyone; this folder is just *my* FIFA World Cup 2026 Fantasy team and the thinking behind it.

| File | What it is |
|------|------------|
| `my_squad.md` | My current 15 players — XI/bench, formation, captain & vice, bank, and which boosters are still unused. Updated after every change. |
| `DECISIONS.md` | Round-by-round decision log: every transfer, captain pick, and booster call, plus *why* (and the moves I rejected). Newest entries are appended each round. |

Read it as a worked example of applying the rules in
[`../data/fantasy_rules.md`](../data/fantasy_rules.md) to the data in
[`../data/`](../data/) — copy the process, not the roster.
